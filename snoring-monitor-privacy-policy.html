Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Snoring Monitor ("the app") is developed by Juris Bregis. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

The app collects the following data solely to provide its features:

  • Audio recordings — the app uses the device microphone to record audio during sleep monitoring sessions. All recordings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to any server. Audio is processed on-device for snoring detection and classification.
  • Session data — snoring event timestamps, duration, intensity levels, snore type classifications, and snore scores. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.
  • Preferences — detection threshold, alarm settings, remedy selections, theme preference, and notification settings. Stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase information — if you subscribe to Snoring Monitor Pro, your purchase is processed by Google Play. We use RevenueCat to verify and manage subscription status. RevenueCat may receive your Google Play purchase token and account identifiers. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Microphone Usage

The app requires microphone access to detect and record snoring events. The microphone is only active during monitoring sessions that you explicitly start. Audio data is processed entirely on your device — no audio is ever uploaded, streamed, or shared with any server or third party.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, camera, contacts, health data, or any other personal data beyond what is described above. We do not use analytics services or tracking SDKs. The app does not display advertisements.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play — for app distribution and payment processing
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management

Data Retention

All locally stored data (recordings, session data, and preferences) remains on your device until you delete individual sessions or uninstall the app. You can delete any session at any time from within the app.

Data Export

The app allows you to export session data as PDF reports or email summaries. These exports are initiated by you and shared using your device's standard sharing mechanism. We do not retain copies of exported data.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
