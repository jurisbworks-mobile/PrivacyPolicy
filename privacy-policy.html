Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Eye Strain Timer ("the app") is developed by Juris Bregis. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

The app collects the following data solely to provide its features:

  • Usage statistics — focus session counts, break counts, and streak data. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.
  • Preferences — timer settings, notification preferences, and language selection. Stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase information — if you purchase Eye Strain Timer Pro, your purchase is processed by Google Play. We use RevenueCat to verify and manage subscription status. RevenueCat may receive your Google Play purchase token and account identifiers. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

The free version of the app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or any other personal data beyond what is described above.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play / Google AdMob
  • RevenueCat

Data Retention

All locally stored data (stats and preferences) remains on your device until you uninstall the app. You can reset stats at any time from within the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
